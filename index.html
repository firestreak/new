Team

Jonathan Thon, PhD

CEO, Scientific Officer, Co-Founder

Jonathan is currently an Instructor at Brigham and Women’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School. Jonathan received his Ph.D. in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology from the University of British Columbia, Canada, where he worked closely with Canadian Blood Services for the improvement of the processing and storage of blood platelets. Jonathan co-developed PBG’s bio-mimetic microfluidic platform to generate human platelets.

Joseph Italiano, PhD

Scientific Advisor, Co-Founder

Joseph is currently an Associate Professor at Brigham and Women’s Hospital, and holds cross-appointments at Boston Children’s Hospital and Harvard Medical School. Joseph received a B.Sc. in Biology from The College of New Jersey and a PhD in Cell Biology from Florida State University. Joseph has over 15 years of experience studying platelet production

Sven Karlsson, CFA, MBA

CFO, Co-Founder

Sven Karlsson has over six years of experience in business development and financial analysis. Prior to joining Platelet BioGenesis, Sven Karlsson worked in Business Development at J.P. Morgan and as a Research Analyst at Seamans Capital Management. Sven received a B.S. in Engineering from Cornell University and an MBA with Distinction from New York University’s Stern School of Business.

Linas Mazutis, PhD

Research Scientist, Co-Founder

Linus Mazutis is currently a Research Fellow at Harvard University and has over eight years of experience developing and applying microfluidic systems to various biological and biomedical applications. Technological innovations developed by Linas Mazutis are currently being used by such companies as Novozymes, RainDance Technologies and BioRad.
